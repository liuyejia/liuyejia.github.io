Biography

I am currently a second-year PhD student in the UC Riverside. My research interest mainly lies in continual learning and efficient AI on distributed systems. Previously, I worked in Amazon and Tencent as a software engineer. Before that, I spent two delightful years in the Simon Fraser University and obtained my Master degree there.
